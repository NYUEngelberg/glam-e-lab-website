Digitisation project planner, handbook and examples

Mathilde Pavis, Andrea Wallace and Sarah Saunders for the National Lottery Heritage Fund

The digitisation project planner will take you through the steps needed to turn your digitisation idea into a defined plan.

Using the digitisation project planner, you will be able to:

  • define your project goals

  • assess what resources you have available

  • identify the scope of your

  • project and create a budget

  • create a plan to find the resources you need

  • make a business case or prepare a funding application
