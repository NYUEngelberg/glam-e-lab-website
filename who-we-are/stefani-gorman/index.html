Stefani Gorman

Student Fellow 2023 (Exeter)
Profile

Stefani is a Research Fellow for the GLAM-E Lab at the University of Exeter (Fall 2023). Her work focuses on the treatment of human remains in US and UK cultural institutions.

Stefani is a Juris Doctor candidate at Seattle University (May 2024). She has a particular interest in international law issues, acting as president of the International Law Society. Stefani previously received a BS from Johnson & Wales University.

The GLAM-E Lab works directly with GLAM institutions to develop open access solutions accessible to the entire GLAM community.
